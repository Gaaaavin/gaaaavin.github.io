I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at NYU AI4CE Lab, advised by Prof. Chen Feng. Before that, I obtained my B.Sc. degree from New York University and NYU Shanghai.

My research interest lies in the intersection of computer vision and robot perception. More specifically, I’m interested in how autonomous agents perceive, forecast, and plan in complex 3D environments through vision.
