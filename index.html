Xinhao Liu

Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at NYU AI4CE Lab, advised by Prof. Chen Feng. I'm currently also an Applied Scientist Intern at Amazon FAR. Before that, I obtained my B.Sc. in Honors Mathematics from New York University and NYU Shanghai.

My research interests lie at the intersection of robotics, computer vision, and deep learning, with a focus on embodied AI in open-world environments. My research focuses on Universal Navigation, where I aim to build embodied intelligence that can go anywhere that humans can go.

Research Keywords: World modeling, Video Understanding, Digital Twins
