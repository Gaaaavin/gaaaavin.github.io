Xinhao Liu (刘歆昊)

I am a senior undergraduate student at NYU Shanghai. My major is Honors Mathematics and Data Science. I'm currently doing my research in the AI4CE Lab, mentored by Prof. Chen Feng.

My research interest is the intersection of computer vision and robot perception. More specifically, I'm interested in how autonomous agents perceive, reason, and predict the environment through vision. My recent projects include topics like visual place recognition (VPR), LiDAR SLAM, and spatial reasoning.
